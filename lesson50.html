Lesson 50: Mindset in Practice

To practice ethical hacking effectively:

  • Create a personal lab (virtual machines, test networks)
  • Use your Python scripts to automate security tasks
  • Scan your own systems and report vulnerabilities
  • Learn from mistakes without harming others
  • Keep improving your knowledge daily
