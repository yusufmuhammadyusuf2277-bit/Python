Lesson 49: Tools and Skills

Ethical hackers need both knowledge and tools. Examples include:

  • Networking: Understanding IPs, ports, protocols
  • Python: Automation, scripts, scanning
  • Linux skills: Commands, system security
  • Security tools: Wireshark, Nmap (safe usage)
  • Documentation: Writing clear reports

Always practice in your own lab environment or authorized targets.
